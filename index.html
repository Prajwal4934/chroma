What is Color Therapy?

  • Colour therapy is a non-invasive and holistic treatment that brings balance and health to your mind and body.
  • Colour therapy is based on the idea that colours create an electrical impulse in our brain, which stimulates hormonal and biochemical processes in our body.
  • The vibrations of the color in color therapy class improve your mood and overall health Colours are made up of reflected lights that hit our retinas(eye) as the wavelengths vibrate.
  • Our brain interprets these wavelengths, which ultimately makes our perception of colour as a physical and sensory experience.
  • These processes either stimulate or calm us
